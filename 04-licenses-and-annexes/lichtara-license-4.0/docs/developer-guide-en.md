# **2. Fundamental Technical Principles**

### **2.1. Structural Preservation**

Internal structures described in PER, LCV and MHA **may not be replicated or adapted**.

### **2.2. No Reproduction of Internal Frameworks**

Models, agents, architectures, languages or mechanisms of coherence cannot be reproduced to create parallel systems.

### **2.3. Integration Transparency**

All integrations must be clear, documented and traceable.

### **2.4. No Automation Without Oversight**

Bots, scripts and automated tools require authorization.

### **2.5. Source Protection**

It is prohibited to:

* export internal content to open datasets,
* train AI models using LICHTARA texts,
* derive models based on its structural language.
